Info and disclaimer

We will not include legendary pokemons for game fairness, as the level of pokemons is static.


We will not include all of the pokemons abilities, just 4 per types, and 2 of each if a pokemon has 2 types. The abilities are included in the pok_moves.csv that we filled in from a real database.


This is not a realistic or faithful representation of pokemon's gameplay, and it surely is inaccurate and false.

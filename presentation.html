Info and disclaimer

We will not include legendary pokemons for game fairness, as the level of pokemons is static and cannot evolve.

We will not include all of the pokemons abilities, just 4 per types, and 2 of each if a pokemon has 2 types. The abilities are included in the pok_moves.csv file extracted from another database.

This is not a realistic or faithful representation of pokemon's gameplay, and it is inaccurate.

All credit goes to Nintendo©, GameFreak© and Nantic©.
